About Lykke og Liten

A unique expertise in sewing and pattern construction makes me skilled in adapting bridal gowns, suits, formal wear, and other clothing used for important occasions such as weddings, confirmations, baptisms, and funerals.

I have a background as a design assistant, salesperson, and education in Fashion Design obtained in Southampton. Therefore, you can be confident that your assignment will be carried out with professional competence and precision. If you have an idea for a design, an oversized dress, or trousers that are too long and need preparation for the big day, do not hesitate to contact me for an obligation-free conversation and guidance.

My focus is on taking the time to understand my customers well. This has resulted in positive feedback on previous assignments. The sewing studio is creative and solution-oriented. Together, we will find the best result for you, whether it's clothing, curtains, or other interior items you need help with.

Environmental concerns are also close to my heart, so I gladly repair small holes, replace zippers, perform other repairs, or alter an old dress.

Lykke og Liten is located in the northern part of Haugesund within the premises of Lie 1899. I am flexible and welcome customers who are home for weekend visits from Bergen or Stavanger. Feel free to share the page and join in supporting local production and craftsmanship.

Best regards, Bergit
